Heaven and Back


by Sean Steinacher

Heaven and Back is a comedic game for the phone or computer in which the player dies and arrives at Heaven’s gates. Unfortunately, a dispute has arisen between the forces of Heaven and Hell over the player’s soul preventing them from directly entering the gates of Heaven. Now that they have been given entrance into Heaven’s waiting area, they must find some means of entering the afterlife and discovering any or each of the three endings that lead to eternity.
Instructions: Heaven and Back is simple and the player should have no trouble finding the different endings. Every door can be entered and every room has something interesting to interact with or a person to talk to that may have information that can help, or at least entertain, the player.
