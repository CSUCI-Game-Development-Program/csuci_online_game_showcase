Shore Cleanin’


by Frances Zercher

The gameplay consists of controlling a young girl who visits a polluted beach and the main objective is to collect all the trash on the sand to restore the beach to its previous state. The interesting thing is that there are many side quests such as retrieving eggs for animals or collecting seashells. To enter a new area of the beach, the player must go to the area where the arrow is pointing.
