Adventure to the Capital

by Ryan Ellis

In this game you take must go on a three-day Journey to the Capital, to find some medicine for your sick mother. With little more than the clothes on your and some food for the journey you must cross the vast forest separating your little village from the Capital.
Instructions: For this game interact with all the NPC’s as it will provide you with not only the context of the story but also show you where you have to go, and what needs to be done.
